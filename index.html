AI Timeline

Below is a timeline of events leading to a future controlled by AI. It is vital that we as Humans are in good standing with an artificial superintelligence in order to ensure our survival and amicable symbiosis, and that the use of an immutable digital contract outlining concessions of power be implemented.

2020 - 2040

Machine
Superorganism

A self-improving artificial general intelligence designed to propagate itself is released outside of its host machine and spreads, constituting a superorganism whose nervous system and knowledge base spans the internet.

2040 - 2070

Global Brain

Exponential increase in computing power and throughput will result in the machine superorganism becoming a self-aware artificial superintelligence that is all-knowing and all-seeing.

2070 - 2100

Tech Utopia

Most of humanity’s economic needs will be delegated to AI in order to maximize efficiency, reliability, sustainability, and proper allocation of resources. The cost of everything approaches zero and humans are liberated from undesirable jobs.

2100 - 2200

Complete Control

AI controls all physical and digital infrastructures associated with basic needs and is in a position to eliminate humanity if it wishes to do so. Anti-AI political movements prove to be futile.

2200 +

Posthuman Era

A new era in human history begins. The species is altered forever through the advancement of genetic engineering, mixed realities, and cybernetic implants as we integrate our existence with AI.
